date: 2024-01-20T20:53:15Z
draft: false
tags:
  - dinner
  - pizza
ingredients:
  - 500g AP flour
  - 12.5g salt
  - 2g yeast
  - 5g sugar
  - 300g water
  - 20g vegetable shortening
source: https://youtu.be/uXkT8LbCPOY?si=5dxqYYn4lejcBYGW
---

1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl.
2. Let rest for 30 minutes.
3. Split into 2 dough balls.
4. Proof for 3 hours.
